## Abstract

In this paper,by using the self-compacting ability of SCC,based on existing construction method of prepacked aggregate concrete,a new construction method of Self-Compacting Concrete filled prepacked rock has been proposed.The prepacked rock body is filled with SCC to form a new type of concrete (RFC,rock filled concrete).An experimental method has been proposed to study the possibility of SCC filled prepacked rock,and to verify the hardening property of RFC.The prepacked rock filling experiment has been carried out,using specially designed low cement dosage SCC.The verification of filling up result and strength testing results demonstrate the practical future of RFC construction method.
