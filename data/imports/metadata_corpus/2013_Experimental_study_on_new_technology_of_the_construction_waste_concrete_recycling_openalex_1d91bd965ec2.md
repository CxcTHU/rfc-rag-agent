## Abstract

Some or all of the natural aggregate could be replaced by the recycled aggregate crushed with the waste concrete.The recycled concrete will be constructed with the recycled aggregate to realize the construction waste resource utilization.But it will bring about the problem of increased costs and secondary pollution.Based on the rock-filled concrete technology,a new technology of recycling of waste concrete was proposed.At first,the waste concrete was simply broken into large diameter aggregate to be prepacked the rock-filled body; and then the self-compacting concrete was poured into the rock-filled body to construct the new recycled rock-filled concrete.The experiment of the filling property of self compacting concrete and the compressive and tensile properties were carried out.Laboratory test results showed that using this technology a dense and high strength concrete structure could be easily constructed due to the self-compacting concrete could be used to fill the pores between the waste concrete.The recycled rock-filled concrete specimen may have a similar strength with self-compacting concrete when the pouring.The new technology of the renewable concrete proposed could realize the renewable resources and utilization of the waste concrete efficiently and economically.
