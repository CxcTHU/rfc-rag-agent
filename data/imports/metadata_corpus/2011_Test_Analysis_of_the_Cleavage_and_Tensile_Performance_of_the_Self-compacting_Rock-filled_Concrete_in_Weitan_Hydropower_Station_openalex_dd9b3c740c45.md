## Abstract

The self-compacting rock-filled concrete is used to construct Weitan Hydropower station.In order to verify the feasibility of this technique the large samples are directly cut from the self-compacting rock-filled concrete on site,then the study on the cleavage and tensile performance is conducted.The results show that the average cleavage and tensile performance is 1.75MPa.It fully meets the requirements of engineering.
