## Abstract

Among the rock-filled arch bam,cofferdam,port,etc,the self-compacting rock-filled concrete (RFC) appears to be a prospect of future development.Tensile performance affects the quality,fractures and deformation of large dam in the action of the hydraulic pressure over a long period of time in the large dam concrete structure.By means of cutting the samples from the RFC test block directly,this test deals with the splitting tension strength and flexural strength of RFC and builds the relationship between the compressive and tensile strength,and gives out the relevant mathematics expression.This research is provided the reasonable mechanics parameter for engineering project.
