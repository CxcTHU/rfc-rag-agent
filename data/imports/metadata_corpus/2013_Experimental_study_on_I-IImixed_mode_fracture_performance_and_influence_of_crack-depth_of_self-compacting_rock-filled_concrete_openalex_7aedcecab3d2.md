## Abstract

An experiment on Ⅰ-Ⅱ mixed mode fracture performance of self-compacting rock-filled concrete was carried out by using large-size single incision four-point shearing beams with three different crack-depth ratios.The fracture mechanism of new technology material was researched and the fracture loads,initial loads,F-δ curve and crack initiation angles were measured in this experiment.The results showed that crack initiation angle increased obviously when crack-depth ratio ranges from 0.4 to 0.8,but it growed slowly when crack-depth ratio ranges from 0.8 to 1.2.
