## Abstract

5 groups of recycled coarse aggregates rock-fill concrete prismoids are made in abandoned rubble,abandoned bricks and abandoned concrete blocks.The influence of recycled coarse aggregates of different types and dosages on the prismoids compressive strength,modulus of elasticity as well as peak strain is investigated.The results indicate that prismoids compressive strength and modulus of elasticity of recycled aggregates rock-fill concrete will be larger with recycled coarse aggregate strength is heightened,but will be diminish with the categories of recycled coarse aggregate are increased;peak strain gets smaller with less types and more uniform distribution of recycled coarse aggregate in the recycled aggregate rock-fill concrete.
