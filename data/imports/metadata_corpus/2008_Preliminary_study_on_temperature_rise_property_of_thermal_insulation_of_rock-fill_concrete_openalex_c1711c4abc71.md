## Abstract

The technique of rock-fill concrete for dam construction is a new dam construction technique with advantages of high construction speed,economic construction cost,environment-friendly construction and easy quality control etc.The temperature rise property of thermal insulation of rock-fill concrete is an important index to study the crack resistance property of the rock-fill concrete dam.A method to combine both the physical test on temperature rise property of thermal insulation of self-compacted concrete and the numerical test on temperature rise process of thermal insulation of rock-fill concrete is adopted herein to study the temperature rise property of thermal insulation of rock-fill concrete,for which a simplified calculation method is put forward as well.
