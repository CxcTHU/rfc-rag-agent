## Abstract

The auxiliary dam of the upper reservoir of Baoquan pumped storage power station is filled with rock-filled concrete, which accelerates the schedule of construction, shortens the duration, reduces investment, and guarantees the quality of the construction effectively. This is the first application of rock-filled concrete in dam filling in hydraulic engineering, and it develops a new technology of using rock-filled concrete to fill dam, which provides valuable experience in the diversity and rationality of hydraulic engineering construction.
