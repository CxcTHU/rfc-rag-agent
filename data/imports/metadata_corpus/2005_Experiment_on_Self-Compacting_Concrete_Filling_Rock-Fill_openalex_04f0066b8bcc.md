## Abstract

The self-compacting concrete, as a kind of high-performance concrete, is widely used at home. So if the self compacting concrete is filled with rock-fill, and its characteristic of high performance, no-vibration,self-compacting,etc. are exerted fully, it will receive excellent rock-fill concrete of comprehensive performance, and it is a kind of promissing new-type concrete.The authors study the flowing and filling about the self-compacting concrete which is filled with rock-fill, and show that it is feasible with self-compacting concrete which is filled with rock-fill. The experiment has reached the anticipated result.
