## Abstract

A new type of concrete named RFC(rock-filled concrete),which was based on the technology of SCC(self-compacting concrete),was developed in 2003,and it has already been employed in hydraulic engineering structures in China.A large scale RFC test block(2.0 m×(1.0 m×1.8 m)) with cold and hot joints was designed and constructed in the laboratory in order to study the integrate properties of RFC.Thirty-nine specimens with different sizes were cut from the different parts of the block,and the mechanical properties and durability of RFC were studied based on the experimental tests.The results suggest that: the prismatic axial compressive strength of RFC is higher than that of SCC used in RFC construction;the cold and hot joints are planes of weakness in the RFC block; and the permeation resistance index of RFC is high enough to satisfy the dam concrete in hydraulic engineering.
