## Abstract

In order to study the behavior of the self-compacting rock-fill concrete beam under static load,two tests of the three-dividing point loading are done.The behavior of the self-compacting rock-fill concrete beam under normal section stressing is analyzed in detail.The experiment proves that the integrity of the self-compacting rock-fill concrete beam is well and the assumption of the horizontal section is suit to the study on the self-compacting rock-fill concrete beam.
